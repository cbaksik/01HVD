Online full text through HathiTrust Emergency Access

As an emergency measure due to COVID-19 restrictions, HathiTrust is opening up the full text for over 2 million copyright-protected items, provided Harvard Library owns a physical copy.

  1. Follow the link from the HOLLIS record (or, if a librarian provides a link, use that).
  2. Once at HathiTrust, use the yellow "LOG IN" button in the upper right corner. Choose Harvard to get to HarvardKey authentication.
  3. You can "CHECK OUT" a book for 1 hour. Your access will renew automatically at the end of that hour unless another user has requested the book.

Free content during pandemic

In response to the Coronavirus outbreak many publishers and vendors are making some or all of their content freely available. We have an evolving list of free content available during the pandemic. The list does not include material that is normally available through regular Harvard Library subscriptions. Please note that availability now will not guarantee availability in the future, and that these free items won't necessarily be found in HOLLIS.
